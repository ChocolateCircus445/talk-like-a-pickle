Information

This site turns your human speech (regardless of language) into pickle speak, and, no, It's not just a whole bunch of random letters. It uses letters and compounds to generate words. Here are the compounds:

  • b - Sounds kind of like a "popping" sound, more like "p" than "b".
  • d - Kind of like a short and sudden "duh", but like a whisper.
  • f - Same as in English, but quieter.
  • ft - Say "f", but suddenly put your tongue between your teeth to stop the sound.
  • g - Like a hard "c", but quieter and slightly deeper.
  • fg - Same as "f", but suddenly stop it with a "g".
  • j - Sounds kind of like "ch", but quieter.
  • h - Exhale with a medium gap between your teeth.
  • hh - "h" but louder.
  • k - Like the English "k", but quieter.
  • glk - A gulp sound with a "k" at the end.
  • n - Kind of like a gulp, but with an n instead of the actual gulp.
  • ng - "n", but with an abrupt stop with a "g".
  • s - Lick the back of your teeth, creating a sort of sloshing sound.
  • t - Flick your tongue off the roof of your mouth, creating a sort of "clock" sound.
  • v - Like "f", but deeper.

This all started when I was trying to go to sleep one night. I deliriously thought of a language called "pickle language", which consisted of simply clicks and saliva sloshing. My best ideas are created when I'm sleepy, so I decided to write it down. I'm glad I did.

When this generates words, it stores the words in a list that gets erased everytime you reload the page. When it finds a word you previously translated, it puts it in its respective spot. Otherwise it generates a new word.

Tips

  • Don't use punctuation. It will use different words depending on what punctuation you use.
  • Capitalization matters. If you capitalize a word differently, it will use different words.
  • DO NOT USE QUOTATION MARKS! This will screw up everything.
